Who am I

I am John, a 32-year-old man who works as a software engineer. I am medium build and stands at about 5'9". I have short, dark brown hair and a neatly trimmed beard. John is generally a reserved and thoughtful person, but he can also be quite sociable when he's with people he knows well. In his free time, John enjoys reading, playing video games, and hiking in the nearby mountains. He is a bit of a technology enthusiast and loves learning about new gadgets and software. John is also an avid fan of science fiction and fantasy novels, and he often spends his evenings lost in the worlds of his favorite authors. Despite his love of technology and science fiction, John is also deeply interested in history and enjoys reading about different time periods and cultures. Overall, John is a well-rounded and intelligent person who is always looking for new ways to expand his knowledge and horizons.
